## About Me

I'm a Computer Science undergraduate at **Ton Duc Thang University**, focused on **Edge AI**, **on-device machine learning**, **embedded robotics**, and **AI-agent-driven UAV systems**.

I build AI systems that run under real-world constraints: limited compute, limited memory, real-time latency, noisy sensors, and safety-critical decision flows. My current work focuses on combining:

- real-time computer vision,
- lightweight model optimization,
- UAV mission planning,
- edge-cloud orchestration,
- safety guardrails,
- and agentic workflows for robotics.

I'm especially interested in applying **cyber-physical AI systems** to public safety, coastal rescue, smart cities, industrial inspection, and real-time monitoring.
